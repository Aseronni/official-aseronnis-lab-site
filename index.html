ABOUT ME

Welcome to Aseronni's Lab

Hey! I'm Aseronni — a 14-year-old indie game developer, 3D modeler, and music composer. This is my corner of the internet where I share the games and projects I'm building from the ground up.

What I Do

  • Game Development: Designing and coding original games in Unity
  • 3D Modelling: Creating assets and environments in Blender
  • Music & Audio: Composing original soundtracks in FL Studio
  • Art & Visuals: Crafting UI, concept art, and animations in Procreate and After Effects

About Me

I've been making games since I was around 8 or 9, and it's still the thing I'm most passionate about. I handle everything myself — from game design and programming to 3D art, music, and storytelling. My biggest project right now is Terraformation Unknown, a sci-fi simulation game I've been developing for a while.

Get In Touch

Take a look around, try out my games, and feel free to leave feedback — I genuinely read it. Always happy to connect with other players and developers!
